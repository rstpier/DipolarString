The idea in one line

Three fields, one medium.

Space is described at every point by three fields. The whole phenomenology — gravity, light, entanglement — follows as regimes of a single transmission-line electrodynamics.

Z
Impedance

Refraction of the impedance field is gravity. An exponential profile Z(r) = Z₀·e^(2GM/rc²) reproduces Schwarzschild at every tested order.

Ω
Vorticity

Cell rotation lowers the local impedance — the frame-dragging analogue, and the door to c_eff > c₀ in the wake.

Π
Polarity

Orientational order of the dipolar gas. Quadrupolar nematic: it carries the tensor sector (+/× gravitational waves) without making the vacuum birefringent.

What would kill the model

Falsification hierarchy

Ordered by accessibility and decisiveness. The first has no adjustable parameter — a ratio of two pure constants.

  1. Shadow deviation δ = +4.63% b_c = e·r_s versus (3√3/2)·r_s for Schwarzschild. Within ~1–1.4σ of the 2022 EHT bounds, on the positive side.
    ngEHT · most accessible
  2. Zero breathing mode A single scalar-mode detection by LVK falsifies the tensor construction.
    LVK · binary
  3. Hard LRD mass cutoff Caustic pile-up below M_crit ∝ ℓ₁², with an obligatory thermal photosphere. No competitor produces a hard cutoff.
    JWST
  4. Non-periodic echoes Spacing exponential in compactness (Δt ∝ e^x/x²), not logarithmic. Outside current quasi-periodic template searches.
    LIGO / Virgo
  5. No PBH evaporation burst No horizon, no late-time thermal flux. A single confirmed detection falsifies.
    HAWC / Fermi · binary
  6. 2PN pulsar deviation Δg₀₀ = −U³/6. Masked by the Lense–Thirring term until the moment of inertia is measured independently.
    PSR J0737−3039 · demoted
